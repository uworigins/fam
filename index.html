About Origins.

ORIGINS is a competitive urban dance family representing the University of Waterloo. We strive to show gratitude and respect to everyone and everything that made us who we are today; hence our mantra: remember your roots.

... in progres..

Mission

Origins focuses on bringing new and exciting opportunities to the Waterloo community.
Here's what we do,

Training

We offer a safe and encouraging environment to train for the intermediate skill level. Our directors aim to bring valuable experiences from cities in and about Waterloo. We believe that opening up our members to different dance styles will build a strong foundation and knowledge, thereby acting as a catalyst to growth.

Compete

Our family participates in various dance compeitions about North America. In the past, Origins has competed in Boston, Toronto and other local cities. We believe the more that our family is on stage, the quicker it can be come a second home. Origins maintains an inclusive policy for compeitions and ensures that you will gain more valuable dance experience.

Open Rehearsals

Origins hosts open rehearsals to help dancers get a feel for the family and ease the transition into a competitive dance team environment. We enjoy seeing new faces at our opens and having you apart of our circle.

Labs

To help encourgage each individual to unlock their full potential, we host internal lab sessions in which dancers are invited to freestyle in a supportive environment and build upon confidence.

Showcases

Helix partners with Origins to provide a platform, "The 7th Dimension", for up and coming choreographers and artists to showcase and share their art to an audience close to home. Coming soon.
